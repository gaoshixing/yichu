藤门国际介绍

IVYGATE INTERNATIONAL
INTRODUCTION

藤门国际教育咨询有限公司始立于2007年10月，属留学教育咨询服务机构。藤门专注于美国高端留学申请，服务项目涵盖美国初高中申请、本科申请、硕博申请及培训学院，致力于美国高端留学，开发独具特色的美国名校申请提前规划项目、名师一对一标准化考试项目培训等。

Ivygate International Education Consulting Co., Ltd. is the premier education consulting firm for Chinese students seeking admissions to top American high-school, undergraduate, and graduate institutions. Founded in 2007, we use a unique approach which aims at producing the strongest applicants. We not only provide application preparation, but supplement the student's education and experiences with individualized project planning and one-on-one exam tutoring. With our team of over sixty former admissions officers and counseling experts from top universities, we are the gateway to an elite education.
