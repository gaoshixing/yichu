    STANDARDIZED TESTS

    TOEFL \ IELTS \ SAT \ ACT等
    目前申请美国高中，学校普遍 要求提供TOEFL和SSAT。

    TOEFL: Although TOEFL exams are held every month, do not take the exam too frequently.

    SSAT: The SSAT is held four times a year, from October to January. As with most standardized exams, it is best not to take the SSAT more than three times.

    Once you receive a TOEFL score of at least 80, it is time to sign up and start reviewing for the SSAT. You should also begin studying for the SSAT if you have not achieved a score of 80 by August; otherwise, there may not be enough time to properly prepare.

    学术成绩

    ACADEMIC PERFORMANCE

    即在校成绩单，是体现学生学术能力的直观材料，也是重要申请材料之一。美国中学申请一般需要最近三年成绩单，均分80分以上。Top前50虽没有明确GPA，但建议申请的学生每科成绩都在B（80-90分）
    While of course a better academic record gives access to higher ranking schools in general, your transcript from the past two to three years will be most relevant to your application.

  • 04

    文书

    ESSAYS

    在申请美国高中留学时，申请文书所要传达的最重要的一个内容即是申请主题。所谓申请主题，即通过挖掘自我生活来寻找素材，再通过素材反映申请者的个性和价值观。
    The application essay (s) aim to get a deeper understanding of you as a person. While common topics include interests, family, and social activities, it is best to choose a topic that will give admissions officers a sense of your personality and show why you are a good fit for the school.

    Pia

    首席美方招生官

    推荐信

    LETTERS OF RECOMMENDATION

    推荐信是从第三者的立场来介绍、展示申请人的综合素质以及优秀品质，这对于学校衡量录取与否是非常重要的。
    For the recommendation letters, choose people who are familiar and have interacted with you academically. The letters are most effective when they contain specific examples which speak to your personal qualities.

  • 06

    面试

    INTERVIEW

    面试作为美高申请者与校方的直接沟通，其重要性不言而喻。在访校面试环节中，学生需要穿着得体，彬彬有礼地与学校招办人员面谈，给学校留下好印象，增加被录取的可能性。
    While the essay (s) provide a good opportunity to show your personality, the interview gives schools an idea of how you think, speak, and interact. Interviewers will take note of not only your English fluency, but also how engaging your answers are. Plenty of strong candidates struggle with the interview because they are nervous or unprepared. You should practice interviews in advance and be prepared for the commonly asked questions.
